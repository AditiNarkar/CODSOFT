RAJASTHAN
Rajasthan is renowned for its stunning architecture, rich history, colorful culture, and natural beauty. Rajasthan is known for its historical hill forts & palaces, it is claimed as the best place for tourism-related to palaces.
MEGHALAYA
Meghalaya's unparalleled natural beauty makes it a highly sought-after holiday destination in India. Major tourist attractions in Meghalaya go far beyond its national parks, natural landforms, waterfalls, towering peaks, caves, etc.
